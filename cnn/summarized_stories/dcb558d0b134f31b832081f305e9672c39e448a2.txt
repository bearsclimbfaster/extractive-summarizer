The newly appointed Japanese ambassador to China has died after taking ill and collapsing in Tokyo late last week. In a brief statement, the Japanese Foreign Ministry confirmed Shinichi Nishimiya, 60, died on Sunday after spending the weekend in hospital because of his "health condition."
