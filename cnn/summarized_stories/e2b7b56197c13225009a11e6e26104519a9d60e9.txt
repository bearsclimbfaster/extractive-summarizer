It is a tale of courage and resilience that rivals the return of Ernest Shackleton and his men from their ill-fated voyage across Antarctica. So, too, should it go to their families and fellow countrymen who have heroically rushed to the rescue. But even as we wait to see if all men make it safely to the top, it is not too early to salute the Americans who pitched in to help as well with the U.S. technology that proved so vital. And UPS did it for free.
