Pakistan may be 95% Muslim today, but Buddhism flourished in Gandhara between the 2nd century B.C. and 10th century A.D., giving rise to a distinct style of Buddhist visual art. Depictions of the Buddha and the concept of bodhisattvas, or "enlightened beings," became the main icons of Gandharan art.
