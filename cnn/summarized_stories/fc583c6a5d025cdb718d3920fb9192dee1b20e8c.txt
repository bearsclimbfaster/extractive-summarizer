Allegations that Italian Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi had sex with a teenage prostitute are "mud" thrown on him by political opponents who want to get rid of him, the scandal-prone politician said. "But this time they've surpassed any limit," Berlusconi said in a statement. This time also they will not succeed," he said in the statement posted on his party's website Saturday. The teen, identified as nightclub dancer Karima El Mahrough and nicknamed Ruby, was 17 at the time of the alleged activity, from February till May. She denied ever having sex with him in an interview recorded Saturday and broadcast Sunday. "If you don't like Berlusconi, then attack him with something else. She had been arrested in May on charges of theft.
